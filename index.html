About CUWMC

The Columbia Undergraduate Wealth Management Club (CUWMC) is a premier student organization dedicated to fostering knowledge and expertise in wealth management, investment strategies, and financial planning.

Founded with the mission to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application, CUWMC provides its members with unique opportunities to engage with industry professionals, participate in case competitions, and develop essential skills for careers in finance.
